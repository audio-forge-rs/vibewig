Voxel

Speak music into existence

Voxel is an experimental system for controlling music production through natural language. You describe what you want to hear, and an AI translates your intent into musical parameters, sending them to plugins running in your DAW.

The system uses a two-phase commit model: changes are staged across all plugins, then triggered simultaneously on beat one. You listen, refine your request, and iterate. The AI remembers the creative context of each version, so you can say "go back to the bright arpeggio" and it knows what you mean.

Built with Rust, CLAP plugins, and OSC. Currently in development.
